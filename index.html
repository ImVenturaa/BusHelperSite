Bus Helper

About Us

Our goal is to give to our users the information about the bus routes and their schedules on all the main bus stops, by doing that we will help them to know the approximate time that they have to be at the respective bus stop, the location of the bus stop and it’s route.

Possibilities

  • Search a route
  • Navigate in the main map
  • Navigate in the individual maps
  • Ask questions
